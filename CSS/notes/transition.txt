transition means going one state to another state
transition: transition-properties/all  transition-time timing-function(ease/ease-in/ease-out/ease-in-out/linear) transition-delay;
eg: transition:all 5s ease 1s

transform property: transform property contains
 -- translateX,translateY,translateZ,translateX
 -- scale,scaleX,scaleY,scaleZ
 -- rotate,rotateX,rotateY,rotateZ
